Our Focus

We focus on a general field in theoretical and computational condensed matter physics. Our research involves using theoretical models and computational simulations to understand the properties of condensed matter systems. This includes studying phases of matter, quantum mechanics, and material properties at the microscopic and macroscopic scale. Our goal is to advance knowledge in this field and contribute to the development of new technologies through our theoretical and computational findings.
