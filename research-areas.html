Our Focus

We focus on a general field in theoretical and computational condensed matter physics, including but not limited to the following:

  1. Topological states and topological phase transitions in periodic and nonperiodic systems.
  2. Predict and investigate novel properties of real materials based on first-principles calculations.
  3. Development of new theoretical approaches and computational algorithms that can describe the electronic properties of solid-state systems.
